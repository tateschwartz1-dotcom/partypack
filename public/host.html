How to Play
🏆 First to 5 points wins.
🎲 Lowest score picks their advantage: choose which side they argue, or whether they go first or second. The other debater picks the remaining option.
🗣️ Each debater gets 30 seconds to argue, then 15 seconds to rebut.
🗳️ Everyone else votes on who won. Tie = half a point each.
🎯 Limited Exchange of Ideas: Two banned entries are dealt before play. Saying one = opponent can press CATCH. Audience confirms. Right catch = catcher wins the round. Wrong catch = catcher loses it.

Debateish needs at least 3 players.
